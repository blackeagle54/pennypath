Can I print these at home?

Absolutely. They're designed for US Letter and work on A4 too. In your print dialog, choose “Fit to page” and turn on background graphics so the colors come through.

Can I use them on my iPad or tablet?

Yes! Import the PDFs into GoodNotes, Notability, Xodo, or any annotation app and write on them with your stylus — reuse them forever, no printing required.

Do I need special software for the spreadsheet?

Microsoft Excel, Apple Numbers, or free Google Sheets all work. Just upload the file to Google Drive and open with Sheets if you don't have Excel.
